# Bright-Coffee-Shop-
Overview of the Case Study:
This project analyzes sales data from Bright Coffee Shop to uncover trends in customer purchasing behavior, store performance, and revenue patterns. The goal was to transform raw transaction data into meaningful insights that can support better business decisions.

The analysis focuses on:
Sales performance over time
Customer purchasing patterns by time of day and day of the week
Product and store performance
Revenue segmentation based on spending behavior
How the Case Study Was Conducted

The analysis was carried out using SQL to clean, transform, and aggregate the dataset.
Key Steps:
Data Preparation
Extracted and transformed date and time fields (day, month, time buckets).
Created categorical variables such as weekday/weekend and morning/afternoon/evening.

Aggregation
Calculated:
Number of sales
Number of products sold
Number of stores
Total revenue per day

Segmentation
Classified revenue into Low, Medium, and High spend buckets.
Grouped data by:
Time of day
Day of the week
Product category
Store location

Analysis
Identified trends in customer behavior and sales performance.
Compared performance across different stores and product categories.

Key Insights
Peak Sales Periods:
Sales are higher during the morning indicating strong customer traffic at this time.
Weekend vs Weekday Behavior:
Weekend transactions differ from weekdays, with high revenue in weekdays.
Product Trends:
Some product categories generate more revenue and have higher demand than others. For example Tea is the most sold across the shops.
Revenue Distribution:
Most transactions fall into the low to medium spend range, with fewer high-value purchases.

Tools Used
SQL (Databricks) – for data transformation and analysis
Miro – for visual planning and diagramming
GitHub – for version control and project documentation
Excel– for charts and insights presentation

Summary
This case study transformed raw coffee shop transaction data into actionable insights by analyzing sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance. The findings can help Bright Coffee Shop optimize staffing, improve product offerings, and increase overall revenue by focusing on peak times and high-performing stores.
